Privacy Policy

Count Runners

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Count Runners ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information when you use our mobile game. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Summary: Count Runners does NOT collect any personal data.

Our game runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information whatsoever.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Count Runners is a fully offline game that does not require an internet connection, user accounts, or any form of registration.

Local Data (Stored Only on Your Device)

The game stores the following data locally on your device using standard system storage. This data never leaves your device:

  • Game progress - Your current level
  • Coins collected - Your in-game currency balance
  • Settings preferences - Sound and haptics toggle states

This data is stored using Unity's PlayerPrefs system and remains entirely on your device. It is automatically deleted if you uninstall the game.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage analytics or tracking data
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No crash reports or diagnostics sent to external servers
  • No cookies or similar tracking technologies

Third-Party Services

Count Runners does not integrate any third-party services, including:

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics platforms
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage or online services
  • No in-app purchase systems

Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to share. We do not sell, trade, or transfer any information to third parties.

Children's Privacy

Count Runners does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. The game is safe for players of all ages as it operates entirely offline with no data collection.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there are no data security risks associated with using Count Runners. All game data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's own security measures.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy regulations, you have various rights regarding your personal data. Since Count Runners does not collect any personal data, these rights are inherently satisfied. If you wish to delete your local game data, simply uninstall the app from your device.
