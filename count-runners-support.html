Count Runners - Help & FAQ

How to Play

Count Runners is a hyper-casual crowd runner game where you guide a group of runners through levels filled with challenges.

  • Swipe left and right to move your crowd of runners
  • Choose doors wisely - each door has a math operation (add, subtract, multiply, or divide) that changes the size of your crowd
  • Avoid enemies that chase and eliminate your runners
  • Collect coins along the way
  • Reach the finish line with as many runners as possible
Tip: Always aim for multiplication and addition doors to grow your crowd quickly. Avoid division and subtraction doors when possible!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my progress?

Uninstalling and reinstalling the game will reset all your progress, including your level and coins. All game data is stored locally on your device.

Q: The game is not loading or crashes on startup.

Try the following steps: (1) Close the app completely and reopen it. (2) Restart your device. (3) Make sure your device has enough storage space. (4) Uninstall and reinstall the game.

Q: How do I turn off sound or haptics?

Go to the settings menu in the game. You can toggle both sound effects and haptic feedback on or off.

Q: Does the game require an internet connection?

No. Count Runners is a fully offline game. You can play anytime, anywhere without an internet connection.

Q: Does the game collect my personal data?

No. Count Runners does not collect any personal data. All game progress is stored locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Q: What devices are supported?

Count Runners is available on iOS and Android devices. The game requires a device running iOS 13+ or Android 7.0+.

Q: I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please email us at nicolajdeveloper@gmail.com with a description of the bug, what device you are using, and any steps to reproduce the issue.
