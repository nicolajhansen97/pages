Privacy Policy for Freezely

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Freezely. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our freezer inventory tracking app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience with our app.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, authentication, and household invitations
  • User ID: Automatically generated unique identifier for your account

User Content

  • Freezer Inventory Data: Item names, quantities, expiry dates, compartment locations, package numbers, and notes
  • Household Information: Household names and member associations
  • Activity History: Logs of items added, updated, or removed

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features like storing your freezer inventory, managing households, and tracking activity
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and secure your account
  • Collaboration: To enable household sharing features with family members
  • Data Synchronization: To sync your data across multiple devices

Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We implement the following security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Row-level security policies to ensure users can only access their own data
  • Regular security updates

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication service. Data is stored on Supabase servers. View their privacy policy at https://supabase.com/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With Your Consent: When you share household data with family members
  • Service Providers: Supabase, which operates under strict data protection standards
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your stored data within the app
  • Deletion: Delete individual items or your entire account
  • Export: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Edit or update your information at any time

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Freezely is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

No Tracking or Advertising

Freezely does not:

  • Track your activity across other apps or websites
  • Display advertisements
  • Sell your data to advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
