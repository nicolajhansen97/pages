Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an alliance?

After signing in, tap the "Create Alliance" button on the home screen. Enter your alliance name and server number. You'll receive a unique join code to share with your alliance members.

How do I invite members to my alliance?

Share your alliance's join code with your members. They can tap "Join Alliance" on their home screen and enter the code to join.

How do I add members to the member list?

You can add members manually by entering their name, or use the OCR upload feature to scan a screenshot of your in-game member list and add multiple members at once.

How do I track VS scores?

Go to the VS Score tab, select an alliance, and add scores for members manually. You can also use the OCR upload feature to scan screenshots of in-game score screens for faster entry.

How do recurring activities work?

When creating an activity, toggle "Make this a recurring activity" and set the interval in hours (e.g., 24 for daily). You can set a specific number of occurrences or choose "Forever" to have activities generated automatically.

How does the Lucky Wheel work?

The Lucky Wheel lets you randomly select a member. You can filter by alliance, rank, or individually exclude members. Spin the wheel and it will land on a randomly chosen member.

Can I use the app on my computer?

Yes! Alliance Center is available as a web app at lastwarac.com. It works on any modern browser and has an optimized desktop layout.

Account & Data

Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • In-App: Go to Settings and tap "Delete Account"
  • By Email: Send a request to nicolajdeveloper@gmail.com

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including alliance memberships and activity history, will be removed.
