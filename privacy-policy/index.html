Privacy Policy

Alliance Center for Last War

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Alliance Center for Last War ("we", "our", "the app", or "Alliance Center") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Alliance Center is an independent companion app for Last War Survival and is not affiliated with the official game developers.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Email address - Used for authentication and account recovery
  • Commander name - Your display name within the app
  • User ID - Automatically generated unique identifier
  • Password - Encrypted and securely stored (we never see your plain password)

2. Alliance Data

  • Alliance names and server numbers you create or join
  • Alliance membership information (who is in which alliance)
  • Member roles and permissions (Leader, Officer, Member)
  • Join codes for alliances you create or manage
  • Alliance creation and management timestamps

3. Activity Data

  • Scheduled activity information (Stronghold, Marshall Guard, Zombie Siege, etc.)
  • Activity types, names, and notes you create
  • Activity scheduling timestamps
  • Your timezone preferences

4. Technical Data

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version you're using
  • Crash logs and error reports (to fix bugs)
  • Basic usage analytics (which features are used)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide app functionality - Store and display your alliances and scheduled activities
  • Authenticate your account - Verify your identity and protect your data
  • Enable collaboration - Allow you to coordinate with alliance members
  • Improve user experience - Understand how features are used and fix bugs
  • Send important notifications - Account security alerts and major app updates
  • Provide customer support - Help you with issues and answer questions
We DO NOT:
  • Sell your personal information to anyone
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing or promotions
  • Track your activity outside our app
  • Show you advertisements

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data is Stored

  • Database: Supabase (enterprise-grade PostgreSQL database)
  • Location: Secure cloud servers with automatic backups
  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest

Security Measures

  • Row-Level Security (RLS): Database policies ensure you can only access your own data
  • Authentication: Secure login system with encrypted passwords
  • Alliance Privacy: Alliance data only visible to alliance members
  • API Security: All communications encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and updates applied promptly

Data Sharing

Who Can See Your Data?

Only You:

  • Your account information (email, commander name)
  • Your personal settings and preferences

Alliance Members:

  • Your commander name (display name)
  • Your role in the alliance (Leader/Officer/Member)
  • Activities you create within shared alliances

Third-Party Services:

  • Supabase - Our database and authentication provider (for infrastructure only)
Important: Your email address is NEVER shared with other users or third parties. Only your commander name (display name) is visible to alliance members.

Your Rights and Choices

You Have the Right To:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of all data we store about you
  • Update Your Data: Edit your commander name, email, or other information in app settings
  • Delete Your Data: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export Your Data: Request a portable copy of your data
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the app at any time

How to Exercise Your Rights:

  • In-App: Go to Settings
  • Email: Contact us at nicolajdeveloper@gmail.com

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account:

  • Option 1 (Instant): Open app → Settings → Delete Account
  • Option 2 (Email): Email nicolajdeveloper@gmail.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"
  • Option 3 (Web): Visit our data deletion page

What Happens When You Delete Your Account:

  • Your account is permanently deleted (cannot be recovered)
  • All your personal information is erased
  • Alliances you created are deleted
  • Activities you scheduled are removed
  • Your membership in alliances is revoked

Timeline: In-app deletion is instant. Email requests are processed within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Alliance Center is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately at nicolajdeveloper@gmail.com and we will delete the account.

Your data may be stored on servers located in different countries. Supabase uses secure data centers with appropriate safeguards to protect your information regardless of location.

  • Inactive Accounts: Data retained indefinitely unless you request deletion
